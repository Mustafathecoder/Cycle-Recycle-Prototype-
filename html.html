About Us

At Cycle Recycle, we make recycling easy and rewarding! Bring in your recyclable materials like plastic bottles, bags, and cans, and earn rewards for helping reduce waste. Together, we can make a real impact on the environment!

How It Works

Recycling is simple and rewarding:

  1. Collect your recyclables like plastic bottles, bags, and cans.
  2. Visit a Cycle Recycle center near you to drop them off.
  3. Earn points based on the quantity and type of recyclables.
  4. Redeem your points for rewards like discounts, gift cards, or donations to environmental causes!

Rewards

For every item you recycle, you'll earn points that can be redeemed for great rewards! Here's a sample of what you can earn:

  • Plastic Bottles: 10 points
  • Plastic Bags: 5 points
  • Cans: 8 points
  • Cardboard: 15 points
  • Paper: 7 points

Redeem your points for gift cards, eco-friendly products, and even donate them to help support environmental charities!
