About Us

Cycle Recycle is a platform where you can recycle everyday items like plastic bottles, bags, cans, and more, while earning rewards for your efforts. Let's work together to create a cleaner, greener planet.

How It Works

Collect recyclables like bottles, bags, and cans.

Drop off your recyclables at a Cycle Recycle location.

Earn points based on the items you recycle.

Redeem points for rewards like gift cards, eco-products, and donations!

Rewards

  • Plastic Bottles: 10 points
  • Plastic Bags: 5 points
  • Cans: 8 points
  • Cardboard: 15 points
  • Paper: 7 points

Redeem your points for exciting rewards or donate them to support environmental causes!
